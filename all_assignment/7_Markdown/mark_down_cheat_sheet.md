# 5- Face of text

**Bold**
*Italic*
***Bold and Italic***

Or you can use these symbols

_ (Underscore)

__Bold__ (two undercore on both side for bold)

_Italic_ (single underscore for italic)

___Bold and Italic___ (Three underscores on each side for bold and italic)
